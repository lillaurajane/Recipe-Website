Spaghetti Bolognese

Our best ever spaghetti bolognese is super easy and a true Italian classic with a meaty, chilli sauce. This pasta bolognese recipe is sure to become a family favourite.

Print

Save

Comment

Step 1

Put a large saucepan on a medium heat and add 1 tbsp olive oil. Reduce the heat and add the 2 onions, 2 carrots, 2 celery sticks, 2 garlic cloves and the leaves from 2-3 sprigs rosemary, all finely chopped, then fry for 10 mins. Stir the veg often until it softens

Step 2

Increase the heat to medium-high, add 500g beef mince and cook stirring for 3-4 mins until the meat is browned all over.

Step 3

Bring to the boil, reduce to a gentle simmer and cover with a lid. Cook for 1 hr 15 mins stirring occasionally, until you have a rich, thick sauce.

Step 4

When the bolognese is nearly finished, cook 400g spaghetti following the pack instructions.

Step 4

Drain the spaghetti and either stir into the bolognese sauce, or serve the sauce on top. Serve with more grated parmesan, the remaining basil leaves and crusty bread, if you like.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves finely chopped
  • 500g beef mince
  • 400g spaghetti
  • Bolognese Sauce

Top Tips

  • Use fresh tomatoes instead of tinned ones to get a richer flavour
  • Experiment with the types of pasta you use
  • The bolognese also works well in a Lasagne
  • Make it vegan by substituting the beef for a vegan mince or brown lentils
